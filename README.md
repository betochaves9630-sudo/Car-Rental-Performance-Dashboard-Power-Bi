#  Car Rental Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

##  Business Problem

Car rental companies must balance **profitability and maintenance costs** across different vehicle brands.  
This project analyzes financial performance to identify **trends, risks, and optimization opportunities**.

---

##  Project Objective

To evaluate how different car brands perform over time by analyzing:

- Monthly profit
- Maintenance costs
- Brand-level performance
- Short-term forecasting

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI  
- Data Modeling  
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- Time Series Analysis  

---

##  Dashboard Overview

This dashboard is divided into three main sections:

### 1. Profit vs Maintenance by Model
- Comparison of total profit and maintenance cost per vehicle model  
- Identification of top-performing and high-cost models  

### 2. Brand Performance Analysis (Scatter + Time Evolution)
- Scatter plot showing relationship between:
  - Profit (X-axis)
  - Maintenance Cost (Y-axis)
- Monthly evolution (Jan–Dec)
- Brand comparison (Toyota, Nissan, Hyundai, Honda)

### 3. Forecasting (15 Days)
- Short-term prediction of profit trends  
- Identification of potential growth or decline patterns  

---

##  Key Insights

- Nissan shows high profit potential but also higher maintenance costs → higher operational risk  
- Toyota maintains stable performance with balanced profit and cost  
- Hyundai presents strong peaks in certain months → seasonal opportunities  
- Honda operates with lower costs but also lower returns  

---

##  Time-Based Analysis

- Performance varies significantly throughout the year  
- Mid-year shows increased volatility across brands  
- Some brands are consistent (Toyota), while others fluctuate more (Nissan)  

---

##  Forecast Insights

- The 15-day forecast suggests a potential recovery after a decline  
- However, variability remains high, indicating uncertainty in short-term performance  

---

## 📁 Dataset

- Simulated dataset representing car rental operations  
- Includes:
  - Revenue  
  - Maintenance costs  
  - Brand and model segmentation  
  - Time variables (monthly/daily)  

---

##  Key Takeaways

- Not all high-revenue brands are the most profitable  
- Maintenance costs play a critical role in decision-making  
- Time-based analysis is essential to detect trends and risks  
- Forecasting helps anticipate short-term performance changes
